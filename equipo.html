Diego Arboleda Jaramillo

CEO/Hevolucion

Chief Executive Officer

Consortia Admin. leader

Internationally recognized for pioneering zero-emission mobility in Latin America. As CEO of Hevolución S.A.S., Diego Arboleda led the deployment of the region’s first 100% renewable hydrogen vehicle and led the development of the Protium plant, the first green ammonia pilot in Colombia. He previously worked in ISA Intercolombia’s investment banking division, supporting large-scale energy infrastructure transactions.

Alejandro Rendón Álvarez

CEO/Hevolucion

Chief Executive Officer

Consortia technical and innovation leader

Recognized by MIT Technology Review as a Innovator Under 35 (2024). Mechanical Engineer with a Master’s in Engineering (Honors), specialized in energy efficiency, renewable energy systems, and green hydrogen. CEO of OPEX S.A.S., a pioneer company in Latin America’s green hydrogen ecosystem. Proven leadership in designing, financing, and executing high-impact industrial decarbonization and clean energy projects in partnership with multilateral agencies, governments, and the private sector.

Alba Luz Hoyos

Chief Financial Officer

Over 20 years of experience in managerial positions in financial and energy companies. Member of multiple boards of directors of major companies in the region.

Mauricio Pérez

Chief Operations Officer

As COO of Hevolucion S.A.S., Mauricio Pérez oversees project execution and risk management. Federico Arébalo

Fertilizers Projects Director

Camilo Mazo

Hydropower & Electrification Director

At Hevolución, he directs the hydropower and electrification component, ensuring efficient energy supply for green hydrogen and ammonia production.

Carlos Mario Ruiz

Instrumentation & Control Lead

At Hevolución, he is responsible for the integration and commissioning of I&C systems for green hydrogen and ammonia facilities, ensuring precision, safety, and operational reliability.

Camila Ocasión

Environmental & Innovation Lead

Chemical Engineer with a Master's in innovation strategy and R&D project management. Camila leads the environmental management systems for the Protium plant, ensuring rigorous compliance in waste handling, water, air, and soil protection. She brings strong expertise in biotechnology, circular economy, and sustainability innovation.

With previous experience leading  innovation projects and coordinating strategic environmental initiatives in companies such as Fabricato and VTSAS, she combines technical knowledge with agile project execution. At Hevolución, she integrates environmental safeguards with strategic innovation for a resilient and sustainable energy transition.

Hader García Almeida

Plant Director

Specializes in process optimization, safety protocols, and operational efficiency in hydrogen-based industrial environments.

Valentina Castillo Cediel

Process & Quality Control Analyst

Cristian Castañeda

Control & Instrumentation Engineer

Pablo Gonzalez Diaz

Hydrogen Projects Engineer

Mechanical Engineer from Universidad de Antioquia with 3+ years of experience in hydrogen energy systems and hydropower-based electricity generation.
